Pernambuco,Brazil

Pernambuco, is a state of brazilian northeastern, situated near the eastern tip of the South American coastline’s bulge into the Atlantic Ocean. It is bounded on the east by the Atlantic, on the south by the states of Alagoas and Bahia, on the west by Piauí, and on the north by Ceará and Paraíba. The state capital is Recife. Home of many cultural manifestations, Pernambuco is the cradle of an artistic expression comprising music and dance, performed mainly during the Recife's Carnival, called Frevo.
